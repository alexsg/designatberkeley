Pioneers in Engineering

Pioneers in Engineering is a STEM (science, technology, engineering and math) outreach group that seeks to create engaging STEM experiences for East Bay students that provide them with the tools, resources, guidance and inspiration to build their own future. PiE provides mentorship to students and also hosts an exciting robotics competition every spring.
