Jacobs Hall

The new home of the Jacobs Institute of Design Innovation will open Fall 2015 and serve as an interdisciplinary space for team-based and project-centric learning. The building will contain design studio spaces and labs with fabrication tools.

Various spaces and labs exist across campus. Each space is typically geared toward a particular design discipline. Not all spaces are open for general use, and many may require training, membership fees, or students to be enrolled in a certain department to access available tools.
